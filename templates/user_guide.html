User Guide

Welcome to our User Guide! Here you'll find instructions on how to use each section of our website.

Creating a Resume

To create a resume, click on the 'Build Your Resume' button on the main page. Fill in the form with your education, experiences, skills, and other relevant information, then click 'Save'.

Generating a Cover Letter

Click the 'Cover Letter Generation' button on the main page, choose an existing resume or create a new one, fill in the job and company details, and voila! Your tailored cover letter will be ready for download.

Tailoring Your Resume

If you want to tailor your resume for a specific job, click on 'Resume Tailoring'. Here you can tweak your resume to better fit the job you're eyeing.

Responding to Application Questions

Click on 'Application Question Responding', choose a resume, and fill in the application question details. This function will help you craft a compelling answer to common application questions.

Job Hunting

Ready to hunt some jobs? Click 'Job Hunting', enter your desired job keywords, and explore the list of job opportunities that pop up.

Resource Center

In the 'Resource Center', you can store and view links to job portals. It's your little corner of resources for the job hunt journey.

Q&A
